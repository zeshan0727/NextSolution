Step-by-Step Jailbreak Process

Preparation Phase

Backup your iPhone using iTunes/Finder. Disable passcode, Find My iPhone, and enable Airplane mode. Charge your device to at least 50%.

Download & Extract palera1n

Download the latest palera1n release from GitHub. Extract the files to a convenient location on your computer.

Enter DFU Mode

Connect your iPhone to computer. Quickly press Volume Up, then Volume Down, then hold Side button until screen goes black. Continue holding Side button + Volume Down for 5 seconds, then release Side button while holding Volume Down for another 10 seconds.

Run palera1n Loader

Open terminal/command prompt in the palera1n folder. Run the command: ./palera1n.sh -c -f (Linux/Mac) or use the provided executable for Windows.

Install Package Manager

After successful exploit, install Sileo or Zebra through the palera1n loader option. This will be your app store for jailbreak tweaks.

Reboot & Test

Reboot your device. You should see the palera1n app. Open it to re-enable jailbreak after each reboot (semi-tethered).

Try different USB cable/port. Reinstall drivers on Windows. Ensure trust this computer alert is accepted.

Package Manager Not Installing

Re-run palera1n with different options. Try alternative package managers like Sileo or Zebra.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this jailbreak untethered?

No, palera1n is semi-tethered. You need to re-run the jailbreak after each reboot using the palera1n app.

Will this work on iPhone XR/XS?

No, palera1n only supports devices with A9 to A11 chips (iPhone 6S to iPhone X).

Can I update iOS after jailbreak?

No, updating will remove the jailbreak. Stay on your current iOS version.

Is it safe for banking apps?

Some banking apps may detect jailbreak. Use tweaks like "Shadow" or "KernBypass" to hide jailbreak.
