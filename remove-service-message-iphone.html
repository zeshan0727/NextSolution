iPhone battery settings showing service message

What's the Battery Service Message?

The "Service" message appears in your iPhone's battery settings when Apple's system detects a non-genuine battery or can't verify battery health data. This often happens after third-party battery replacements, even with high-quality batteries.

In this tutorial, you'll learn how to use 3uTools to remove this warning message and restore proper battery health percentage readings—without needing Apple's proprietary tools.

Key Benefits of This Method

Important Warning

This method modifies system data. While generally safe when done correctly, there are risks:

  • Always back up your iPhone before attempting
  • Only use with genuine or high-quality replacement batteries
  • Don't artificially inflate battery health percentages
