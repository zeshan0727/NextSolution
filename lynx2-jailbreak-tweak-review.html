Unlock complete control of your iPhone with this all-in-one jailbreak customization tool.

Lynx 2 main settings panel with all customization options

What is Lynx 2?

Lynx 2 is a feature-rich jailbreak tweak that allows you to fully customize your iOS device. Compatible with iOS 14 – iOS 16, this tweak offers system-wide tweaks and per-app customizations from one clean UI.

Whether you want to change lockscreen elements, edit status bar icons, disable camera UI clutter, or manage Safari tweaks – Lynx 2 can do it all.

Top Features of Lynx 2

Status bar customization options
Lockscreen modification settings
  • System: SpringBoard tweaks, folder layout control, app hiding
  • Status Bar: Hide Wi-Fi, battery %, clock, VPN, and more
  • Lockscreen: Customize Face ID, charging view, and notifications
  • Control Center: Remove blur, hide tiles, adjust access
  • Dock: Change background blur, remove reflections
  • Privacy: Hide screenshot previews, disable clipboard alerts

Per-App Customization Modules

Safari interface customization options
Camera app modification settings

One of Lynx 2's strengths is its ability to apply settings for specific apps. This means cleaner, faster, distraction-free app usage tailored to your preferences.

  • Safari: Remove share buttons, edit search bar layout
  • Camera: Mute shutter, hide UI buttons
  • Messages: Disable avatars, hide pinned chats
  • Photos: Lock albums, disable metadata sharing

Where to Download Lynx 2

You can purchase the tweak from the official Havoc repository. This guarantees support, updates, and safe installation.

Official Repo: https://havoc.app/package/com.mtac.lynx

Beware of Piracy Repos

Free piracy repos may host cracked versions of Lynx 2, but using them poses serious risks:

  • Outdated or broken versions that cause crashes
  • High chance of spyware, malware, or data leaks
  • No developer support or future updates

Always use official Cydia sources like Havoc, Chariz, or Dynastic to support jailbreak developers.

Summary

Lynx 2 is undoubtedly one of the best jailbreak tweaks for iPhone and iPad. It replaces dozens of smaller tweaks with one modular, efficient interface. For users who want complete control without clutter, it's a must-have.
